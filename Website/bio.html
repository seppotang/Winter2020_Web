About Me

Norm Andresen has been playing bass and guitar for 25 years, and is currently working on several projects, as well as teaching bass and guitar out of his home in Ferndale, MI and throughout Metro Detroit. A New York native, Norm has found the Detroit music scene to be amazing in it’s talent, diversity, and opportunities, which led him to found and run the independent record label Storm Records from 1993-2005.

Current Projects

    Recording solo acoustic songs with Steve Presti at Black Sheep Recording.
    Writing/recording with Britney Bockelman-Maddern.
    Playing bass with McLaughlin’s Alley.
    Playing bass regularly at Our Lady of the Woods church.
    Teaching bass and guitar lessons.

Previous Projects

Over the years, Norm has had the fortune of performing with many talented artists, including Alicia Warrington(fudgeGUN, Kelly Osbourne), Nicole Tindall (11:11, George Clinton) and Stephanie Loveless.

    2003 - 2011 SRB - play bass, backup vox
    2010 JonGen ( FB ) - playing bass, backup vox
    2006-2011 Steffie Loveless and the Dirty Virgins - bass
    2002-2003 Whiskey101 - played bass, vox with this cover band
    1999-2001 Various solo projects/Swallowhole - guitar/bass/vox
    1999-2002 11:11 - played bass, toured East Coast US with this electronica/groove band
    1998 fudgeGUN - play bass, toured East Coast US with this post-punk band
    1992-1996 Nativ Rage - played bass, toured Midwest US with this indie rock band
